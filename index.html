How do you get good at HTML?

Here are some(but not all) things you need to do to exceed in HTML:

  • Do some courses that teach HTML.
  • Always practice what you've learned.
  • It is most important to stay patient!

How do you maintain your focus when learning HTML?

  1. Taking regular breaks.
  2. Stay away from distractions such as a smartphone.
